About me

Hey! I'm Valeria, an artist and aspiring game programmer from Bogotá, Colombia. I studied art at Los Andes University in Colombia and specialize in electronic media. I personally enjoy video and sound editing, but I also have experience in performance, animation and installation art. I am currently studying Game Programming at St. Lawrence College in Cornwall, Ontario. You can click here to check out the courses I'm taking this semester.

In my free time I enjoy drawing on my iPad Pro while listening to true crime stories, podcasts or audiobooks. I also make sterling silver jewelry and sell my pieces in my website, Oh My Deer Joyeria.

For more information about my artistic career, you can download my Artist Profile.

For more information on St. Lawrence College, you can click the logo.
